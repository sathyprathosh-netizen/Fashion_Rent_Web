The Philosophy

Why We Rent

LUXÉ was founded on a conviction that world-class fashion — the kind that commands rooms, freezes time, and becomes memory — should not be gatekept by permanent ownership.

In 2018, our founders asked a simple question: what if the world's most beautiful garments could be shared, worn, loved, and returned — creating more joy, less waste, and a more inclusive luxury experience?

The answer is LUXÉ.

500+ Couture Pieces

12k+ Happy Members

"To rent is not to compromise.
It is to choose experience over possession.

It is to say: I deserve to feel extraordinary,
and I deserve to feel it again and again."

— LUXÉ Founding Manifesto, 2018
Responsibility

Fashion without Consequence

Every time a LUXÉ piece is rented rather than manufactured anew, we collectively reduce textile waste, carbon emissions, and the environmental cost of fast fashion.

Our care process uses only certified eco-friendly cleaning agents. Our packaging is fully recyclable. Our ambition: net-zero by 2028.
