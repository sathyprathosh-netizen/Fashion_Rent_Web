The Process

Effortlessly Simple

World-class fashion. Zero friction. Maximum elegance.

01

Discover & Browse

Explore our curated collection of 500+ runway and red-carpet fashion pieces. Filter by category, designer, season, or occasion. Each piece is photographed, documented, and ready for you.

02

Reserve Your Piece

Select your size, choose your rental dates, and request the piece. Our team reviews each request personally to ensure the perfect fit and experience. Members receive priority confirmation.

03

White-Glove Delivery

Your piece is impeccably steamed, packaged in our signature LUXÉ box, and delivered by a dedicated courier on your chosen day. We also include a garment bag, care instructions, and return packaging.

04

Wear. Own the Room.

Attend your event. Walk that aisle. Command that boardroom. This is your moment — dressed in the finest fashion the world has to offer. Look extraordinary. Feel extraordinary.

05

Effortless Return

We schedule collection at your convenience. No cleaning required — our master couturiers handle everything. The piece is restored, preserved, and ready for its next chapter.

Damage Policy

Minor wear is expected and covered. Significant damage is assessed against the deposit. We're transparent — no surprise charges.

Cancellation

Cancel up to 48 hours before delivery for a full refund. Within 48 hours, 50% of the rental fee applies.

Late Returns

An additional day's rental fee applies per day of delay. Our team will reach out proactively if you need an extension.

Sizing Guarantee

If the piece doesn't fit, we'll exchange it within 24 hours — no questions asked. Your comfort is our priority.

Ready to Begin?

Browse our curated selection and wear something extraordinary at your next event.
